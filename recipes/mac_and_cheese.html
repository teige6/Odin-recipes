Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese has been a staple food in America. It's creamy and cheesy attributes make it irresistable.

Ingredients

  • Elbow pasta
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • butter

Steps

  1. Step 1: Boil water in a pot on the stove.
  2. Step 2: Add elbow pasta to the boiling water.
  3. Step 3: Once shells are soft and cooked, drain water
  4. Step 4: Add cheese to the drained pasta
  5. Step 5: mix and enjoy!
