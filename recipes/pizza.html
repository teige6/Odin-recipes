Pizza Recipe

Pepperoni Pizza

Pizza is a worldwide dish. It is known in many homes and is a easy dish to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • marinara sauce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Step 1:Flatten dough to make a flat circle.
  2. Step 2: Apply marinara sauce lightly to the dough,leaving the crust exposed.
  3. Step 3: Sprinkle shredded cheese in the same areas as the marinara sauce.
  4. Step 4: Apply Pepperoni in areas of your liking.
  5. Step 5: put in oven at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.
